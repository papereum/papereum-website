Art installation

How Google pronounces Papereum

Papereum is a project with the goal to create an art installation in the form of a pyramid, symbolising the growing interest in cryptocurrency. In other words, Papereum will bring cryptomania into the physical world.

The installation will be exhibited in contemporary art galleries worldwide during summer/autumn 2018. After that any token holder can receive parts of the installation in exchange for this share of Papereum, thus getting a valuable piece of art.

Figure 1. Initial concept sketch

The installation to be comprised of seven layers assembled from exactly 200 boxes of cryptomania. And every box will contain exactly 500 uniquely numbered sheets of paper that can be exchanged 1 to 1 for Papereum token.

Figure 2. Prototype of Papereum price screen

One of the components of the installation is a tiny computer with the display which shows Papereum price index, historical chart and market cap based on data from exchanges.

What is cryptomania box?

The cryptomania box is one building block of the pyramid that is made of 200 such blocks with the dimensions:

  • Height: 14 cm (5.5 inches)
  • Width: 22.5 cm (8.86 inches)
  • Depth: 15 cm (5.9 inches)

Figure 3. Cryptomania box

Cryptomania box can be demonstrated by hanging it or by placing it to fit in the space available. NB! Different wall materials require different types of fasteners. You need to use fasteners suitable for the walls in your home.

Every box consists of exactly 500 consecutively numbered sheets of paper. Each piece is numbered manually using automatic numbering machine Reiner B6. The number is comprised of six digits, typed using figure style Antique, figure size 4.5mm, black ink No 200182-000. All numbers are unique and do not repeat in between the boxes.

The entire collection includes 100 000 sheets. All of them are numbered, photographed and posted on Instagram. At any time you can check them here https://www.instagram.com/papereum/.

After the installation has travelled around the world, it will be taken apart and the parts will be sent out to token holders, honoring memory of this fantastic period of cryptocurrency mania of 2018.

Most expensive art

How "Salvator Mundi" Went From £45 to $450 Million in 59 Years:

  • 1958 - Salvator Mundi pops up at a Sotheby's London auction on June 25, 1958. Attributed to Boltraffio, who worked in da Vinci's studio, it sells for £45 to someone named "Kuntz."
  • 2005 - The canvas surfaces again at an American estate sale. New York art dealer Alexander Parish purchases it for another bargain price of $10,000.
  • 2013 - Having authenticated the work as a bona fide Leonardo, Parish and a consortium of fellow dealers sell it to "freeport king" Yves Bouvier in a private Sotheby's sale for a cool $75–80 million. Later that year, Bouvier turns around and sells it for $127.5 million to the Russian billionaire Dmitry Rybolovlev.
  • 2017 - Rybolovlev puts the painting up for sale at Christie's. It fetches $450.3 million.

FAQ

How to get the whole cryptomania box?

When sheets of paper are returned to the token-holders, some boxes will be taken apart. To get the whole cryptomania box, you need to own at least one "full set" of tokens. This is how you can get it:

  • Purchase 500 tokens during the Early Sale and receive 150 tokens as a bonus.
  • Buy tokens at any time of sale, if more than 500 tokens remain unsold.
  • Buy 500 tokens at any cryptocurrency exchange after token allocation and listing on token exchanges.

How delivery of my part of installation will be arranged?

Starting December 1, 2018 every Papereum token holder will be able to claim his or her collection piece in exchange for available tokens:

  • If number of tokens is 500 or more, full cryptomania box will be sent to the token holder. Remaining tokens will be exchanged for half-boxes or numbered sheets. In this case, the delivery is carried out by DHL, UPS, FedEx or TNT courier service.
  • If number of tokens is less than 500, but is 10 or more, token holder will receive consecutively numbered sheets collected in 10 pieces. The delivery will also be carried out by courier service.
  • If number of tokens is less than 10, numbered sheets will be delivered in an envelope as registered letter.

How to get bonus token?

The minimum amount of PPRM tokens you need to buy in order to receive 1 PPRM token as bonus:

  • During the Early sale: 10 tokens.
  • During the 3-week Bonus sale: 15 tokens.
  • During the 2-week Bonus sale: 20 tokens.
  • During one-week Bonus sale: 34 tokens.
